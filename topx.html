Independent R&D

The TopX ecosystem

Manufacturing software first — inspection analytics, vision training, and engineering knowledge systems — plus independent research in AI agents, blockchain, and algorithmic trading since 2013.

The Mission

Intelligent infrastructure that can measure, decide, adapt, and scale

Since 2013, TopX has been my platform for building intelligent systems at the intersection of AI, blockchain, algorithmic trading, and advanced automation. The work spans decentralized infrastructure, machine learning, computer vision, financial modeling, and Web3 application development.

I design automated trading strategies using ThinkScript, PineScript, and Python — market-responsive algorithms, signal-generation logic, and backtesting frameworks that evaluate performance, risk, and repeatability across changing market conditions. On the blockchain side, I research and prototype decentralized applications, tokenization systems, NFT marketplace components, and Ethereum-based architectures, with a focus on secure smart-contract design, scalable system architecture, and cross-contract communication.

Alongside this work I lead advanced vision and automation initiatives at Universal Instruments — a rare bridge between real-world industrial automation and emerging computational technologies. Rlab, my founder-led software and engineering lab, gives this work a home: practical prototype systems across AI agents, manufacturing data systems, financial modeling, blockchain engineering, automation tooling, and technical documentation.

Knowledge Chat

  • Local RAG over Word/PDF libraries — upload, index, and chat with documents using streaming answers (SSE) and conversation history.
